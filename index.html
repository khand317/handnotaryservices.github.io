Services

Flexible mobile notary services designed for real life — whether you’re at home, at work, in the hospital, or helping a loved one in custody.

General Mobile Notary

Power of Attorney, wills, affidavits, titles, bill of sale, school forms, and more. I travel to your home, office, coffee shop, or neutral location.

I-9 Verification

Section 2 completion for remote employees. Quick 10–15 minute appointments with clear instructions for both you and your employer.

Bedside notarization for patients and families, including healthcare directives and POA. Coordinated respectfully with staff and family schedules.

Jail / Detention Center Notary

Document execution at local jails and detention facilities for POA, affidavits, and other legal forms. Clear communication before and after the visit.

Document Courier

Optional pickup / drop-off of signed and notarized documents to overnight shipping locations or designated offices.
